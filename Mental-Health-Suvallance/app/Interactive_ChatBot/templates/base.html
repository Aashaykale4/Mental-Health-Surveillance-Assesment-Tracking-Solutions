This stress test uses the Perceived Stress Scale (PSS). It forms part of a
wider-ranging self-assessment undertaken by participants
of the Be Mindful program.
How you will Track your problem and their Solution
The Perceived Mental Stress Scale measures the degree to which situations in your
life are perceived as stressful. The series of questions have been designed
to assess how unpredictable, uncontrollable, and overloaded you feel
your life to be, as well as direct questions about your current
level of experienced mental stress for Wellbeing.



The Survey questions ask about thoughts and feelings during the last month.
They are easy to understand and are of a general nature.
This makes the test relatively free from bias for
or against any specific subpopulation group.
